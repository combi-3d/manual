    Operation

    See Sci Rep (2021)11:13108, Suppl. Fig. S4 for detail.

    1. Connect camera and sensor to the controller box.
    2. Place the sensor unit under the handle.
    3. Expose blockface as a start position.
    4. Turn ON camera controll (Red button).
    5. Turn ON HOLD button. This means half-push of the shutter button on the camera (fix focus).
    6. Capture a scale by pushing "RELEASE" button.
    7. Slice and image blockface serially.

    NOTE: Shutter is released when the handle is going back. Thus, move the handle slowly and carefully when going back. At the magnification 1 - 3x, you do not need care about stability. However, at the highest magnification (5x), blur-free imaging is possible, but very careful manual operation is required.
